## 3. Onboarding Process

### Your First Day

**Before Arrival**
- You will receive an email with parking information and office directions
- IT will provide your laptop and login credentials
- Your workspace will be prepared and ready
- Welcome package will be on your desk

**First Day Schedule**
- 9:00 AM - Arrival and check-in at reception
- 9:30 AM - Meet your manager
- 10:00 AM - HR orientation
- 11:00 AM - IT setup and access
- 12:00 PM - Team lunch
- 2:00 PM - Department introduction
- 3:00 PM - System training
- 4:00 PM - Meet your buddy

### First Week Activities

- Complete mandatory training modules
- Meet your team and key colleagues
- Review your job description and responsibilities
- Set initial goals with your manager
- Complete onboarding paperwork
- Attend company overview presentation
- Get familiar with tools and systems

### First 30 Days

- Complete all onboarding modules
- Meet with department heads
- Attend company meetings
- Establish regular 1-on-1s with manager
- Start initial projects
- Provide feedback on onboarding experience

### First 90 Days

- Complete probationary period requirements
- Full performance expectations established
- Skills assessment completed
- Full integration into team
- Formal 90-day review with manager

## 4. Code of Conduct

### General Conduct

All employees are expected to:
- Treat each other with respect and dignity
- Maintain a professional demeanor
- Follow all company policies
- Report policy violations
- Support company objectives
- Maintain confidentiality as appropriate

### Professional Behavior

- Dress code: Business casual for office, varies by role
- Punctuality: Arrive on time for work and meetings
- Communication: Use professional language in all interactions
- Attendance: Regular attendance is expected
- Performance: Maintain high standards of work quality

### Workplace Safety

- Report hazards immediately
- Comply with safety procedures
- Use protective equipment as required
- Never work under the influence of drugs or alcohol
- Immediately report accidents or injuries

### Harassment and Discrimination

Our company has a zero-tolerance policy for:
- Harassment based on protected characteristics
- Bullying or intimidation
- Discrimination
- Sexual harassment
- Hostile work environment

All incidents should be reported to HR immediately.

### Confidentiality

Employees must:
- Protect confidential business information
- Not disclose proprietary information
- Secure documents and data
- Follow data protection regulations
- Maintain client confidentiality

- Salaries are determined by role, experience, and market rates
- Annual reviews may include salary adjustments
- Performance bonuses are offered in some departments
- Stock options available for eligible employees

### Health Insurance

- Comprehensive medical coverage effective on day 1
- Dental and vision plans available
- Employee pays 20%, company covers 80%
- Dependent coverage available
- Prescriptions covered with copays

### Retirement

- 401(k) plan with company match (up to 4%)
- Immediate eligibility
- Vesting schedule: 25% per year over 4 years
- Financial planning resources available

**Vacation**

- 10 days per year

**Holidays**

**Parental Leave**
- 12 weeks for primary caregiver
- 8 weeks for secondary caregiver
- Paid at 100% salary

### Wellness Programs

- Gym membership reimbursement
- Mental health counseling (EAP)
- Wellness workshops
- Preventive health screenings
- Fitness challenges with prizes

## 6. Communication Policies

- **Email**: Primary business communication
- **Slack**: Quick messages and team collaboration
- **Phone**: For urgent matters
- **Meetings**: Scheduled discussions and brainstorms

## 8. Security and Data Protection

### Password Security

- Strong passwords required (12+ characters, mixed case, numbers, symbols)
- Change passwords every 90 days
- Never share passwords
- Use password manager
- No writing down passwords

### Device Security

- Lock your computer when away
- Enable screen lock after 5 minutes of inactivity
- Use VPN for remote connections
- Keep software updated
- Report lost or stolen devices immediately

### Information Security

- Classify data appropriately
- Encrypt sensitive information
- Follow data access controls
- Report suspicious emails
- Participate in security training

### Privacy

- GDPR compliant policies
- CCPA compliant policies
- Customer data protection required
- Data retention policies enforced
- Privacy incident reporting

## 9. Remote Work Guidelines

### Work from Home Eligibility

- Generally approved for certain roles
- Manager approval required
- At least 3 months employment required
- Must maintain productivity

### Remote Work Arrangements

**Full Remote**
- Work from home 5 days a week
- Available for occasional in-office meetings
- Equipment provided by company

**Hybrid**
- 2-3 days office, 2-3 days remote
- Flexible scheduling with manager
- Core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM
- Specific team requirements may apply

### Home Office Setup

- Ergonomic chair and desk provided
- Monitor and keyboard supplied
- Home office allowance: $500
- Internet requirements: 25+ Mbps
- Quiet workspace needed

### Expectations

- Maintain professional appearance on video calls
- Participate actively in team events
- Report hours worked accurately
- Communicate availability clearly
- Ensure work-life balance
